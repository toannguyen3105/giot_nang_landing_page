Usage:

Step 1: Use exfoliating salt by mixing it with water or fresh non-sugar milk until it thickens and apply directly to the skin as a mask, wait for 10~15 minutes. While waiting, you can massage softly.

Step 2: Wash your face/body with water then using Herb white silage powder in the same way with Exfoliating salt.
